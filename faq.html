What is the address?

It's in Downtown Berkeley, CA.

Who is organizing the event?

A group of researchers, founders, and writers in the bay who think that bringinig together people from around the world interested in solving this problem is pertinent!

Who can attend the symposium?

Anyone working on dating apps, writing and thinking about love and relationships, and think that we can make way better technology for this and that it is really important are exactly who we want to come!

Can I present my research or project?

Yes! Sign up on our form and let us know you want to present.

Is accommodation provided?

Some limited rooms are available at Light Haven for people who want to be in the action! If you pay for a room, attendance is free as well. Otherwise, you can stay anywhere in the Bay Area, and come to Light Haven during the day to attend.

Is there a virtual attendance option?

We don't have any plans for that, but we will have a discord channel you can join to have a way to connect with other people comign to the event. TBD if we will film any of the talks or discussions.

How much is a ticket?

$30 per day to get access to Light Haven, you can pay for the days you want to attend

Can I come for just part of the event?

Sure! Come to as many or as few events as you want. We will have many events which may interest many different types of people. You can only come one day, or to one event, or to as many as you like. You pay for the day to attend.
